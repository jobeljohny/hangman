GAMEPLAY

Your goal is to guess the movie title within 60 seconds. Each round consists of a different movie title to guess. Successfully guessing the movie will take you to the next round.

You start with 6 lives. Each incorrect guess will deduct one life. If you run out of lives before guessing the movie, the game will end.

The game will display a series of underscores representing the letters in the movie title. To guess a letter, simply press the corresponding key on your keyboard.

If the letter you guessed is present in the movie title, it will be revealed in the correct positions in the title. If the letter you guessed is not in the movie title, one life will be deducted, and you will be notified of the incorrect guess.
